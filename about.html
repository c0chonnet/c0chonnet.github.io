I am Kira, a self-taught artist born in 1995. As a child, I was obsessed with ancient Egypt, which inspired the nickname "vaenaton," meaning "the only one for Aton." At the age of 15, I started painting on my computer, but after the first plein air sessions in Pskov, my hometown, I turned to traditional art.


I have never been a full-time artist. My career began in 2016 from a marketing role, and within a couple of years, I used this experience to transition to data field. Constantly seeking to connect my interests, I earned a master's degree in Digital Humanities in 2021. During my master's, I taught Digital literacy at the university, where I guided students' data projects and created a lesson on building simple websites for an online course. By making this page, I aim to combine all my experiences.

I primarily work with acrylics, gouache, and oil pastels, drawing inspiration mainly from everyday life observations, emotional experiences, and memories. Since I began drawing nude figures with ink at life drawing model sessions in 2022, I have increasingly incorporated ink into my work. At the end of 2024, I released my first short game in the graphic style I developed. Tools I use at work include Python, SQL, Javascript and more, and I am always eager to apply these skills to developing interactive media projects. I am currently based in Estonia (Tartu, Tallinn). My creative experience, besides painting and drawing, includes editorial and web illustration, merch design, street and textile art, and identity development.
